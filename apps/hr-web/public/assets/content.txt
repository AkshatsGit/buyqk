CHAPTER 1

WHY THIS DOCUMENT EXISTS

Every startup says:

“We’ll give ESOPs.”

At BuyQK, we believe ownership deserves clarity.

This document exists to explain:

• Why BuyQK believes in ownership.

• How ownership will be viewed.

• What values are expected.

• How long-term contribution matters.

• How future ownership opportunities may be evaluated.

This document is built upon one simple philosophy:

Ownership should never be promised casually.

Ownership should be earned.

CHAPTER 7

Ownership Is Earned, Never Promised

At BuyQK, we believe ownership represents one of the highest forms of trust an organisation can extend to an individual.

For this reason, ownership is never used as a recruitment incentive, a negotiation tool, or a substitute for meaningful contribution.

Instead, ownership is viewed as recognition for individuals who consistently demonstrate the ability to create long-term value for the company.

Every future ownership decision will be guided by merit, integrity, commitment, and the long-term interests of BuyQK.

No designation, seniority, or verbal discussion automatically creates a right to ownership.

CHAPTER 14

What This Document Is Not

To avoid misunderstandings, it is important to understand what this document does not represent.

This document is not:

* An Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP).
* A share certificate.
* An equity grant.
* A partnership agreement.
* A legally binding promise of ownership.
* A guarantee of future equity.
* A replacement for any future legal documentation.

Any future ownership opportunity will only arise through separate agreements, approved company policies, and the applicable legal framework in force at that time.

CHAPTER 15

Why We Don’t Promise Ownership Immediately

Many early-stage startups promise equity before they have a clear legal structure, governance process, or long-term understanding of team contributions.

At BuyQK, we choose a different path.

We believe ownership should be based on sustained contribution, integrity, and long-term commitment—not simply on the date someone joins or the title they hold.

Making promises too early can create unrealistic expectations and unfair outcomes for both individuals and the company.

Instead of promising ownership, we promise fairness, transparency, and an evaluation process based on merit.

Our goal is to ensure that ownership, when introduced, reflects genuine value creation rather than short-term association.

⸻

CHAPTER 16

Why We Use Vesting

Ownership is designed to reward long-term builders.

A vesting schedule encourages consistency, accountability, and alignment between individual contributions and the long-term success of the company.

Rather than receiving ownership immediately, contributors gradually earn it over time through continued involvement and meaningful impact.

This approach protects both the company and its builders by ensuring that ownership reflects sustained commitment rather than a single moment in time.

⸻

CHAPTER 17

Why We Use a One-Year Cliff

The intended one-year cliff serves as an initial period during which both the individual and BuyQK can evaluate whether there is a strong long-term fit.

During this period, we assess factors such as:

* Commitment.
* Professionalism.
* Reliability.
* Collaboration.
* Performance.
* Alignment with BuyQK’s values.

A cliff is not intended as a barrier.

It is a mechanism to ensure that future ownership opportunities are reserved for individuals who demonstrate genuine dedication and contribute meaningfully over time.

Both the company and the individual benefit from establishing a strong foundation before any long-term ownership begins to vest.

CHAPTER 18

Future Company Incorporation

BuyQK is currently operating as a sole proprietorship during its early stage of development.

As the company grows, it is our intention to adopt an appropriate corporate structure that best supports our long-term vision, business operations, governance, fundraising, and employee ownership initiatives.

Following incorporation and completion of all applicable legal and regulatory requirements, BuyQK may establish formal ownership programs, including Employee Stock Option Plans (ESOPs) or other long-term incentive structures, subject to applicable laws and approvals.

Until such time, this document should be understood as a statement of BuyQK’s philosophy and intent rather than a legal instrument granting ownership.

⸻

CHAPTER 19

Future Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP)

BuyQK believes that the individuals who dedicate themselves to building the company over the long term should have the opportunity to participate in the value they help create.

Accordingly, once the company is legally eligible and operationally prepared, BuyQK intends to evaluate the introduction of a formal Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP).

Any future ESOP will be governed by:

* Applicable laws and regulations.
* Company policies.
* Board and shareholder approvals (where applicable).
* Individual performance and contribution.
* Vesting schedules.
* Business requirements.

The exact structure, allocation methodology, eligibility criteria, and terms of any future ESOP will be communicated separately through formal legal documentation.

CHAPTER 20

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Am I guaranteed ESOPs by joining BuyQK?

No.

Joining BuyQK does not automatically entitle any individual to receive equity or stock options.

Future ownership opportunities, if introduced, will be evaluated based on contribution, long-term commitment, company policies, business requirements, and the applicable legal framework.

⸻

2. Can interns become eligible for future ownership?

Potentially, yes.

BuyQK values contribution over designation.

Exceptional interns who continue contributing over the long term and become integral members of the organisation may be considered for future ownership opportunities under the same guiding principles as other team members.

⸻

3. What happens if I leave BuyQK?

If your association with BuyQK ends before any formal ownership program is introduced, no ownership rights arise under this document.

If a formal ESOP or ownership program exists in the future, your rights and obligations will be governed exclusively by the applicable legal agreements and company policies in force at that time.

⸻

4. Can future ownership allocations change?

Yes.

Business priorities evolve, company structures change, and organisational needs develop over time.

Future ownership decisions may be adjusted based on performance, leadership, business requirements, investment rounds, legal compliance, and other relevant factors.

⸻

5. Does performance matter?

Absolutely.

BuyQK believes ownership should reflect meaningful contribution rather than tenure alone.

Performance, accountability, leadership, innovation, collaboration, integrity, and long-term commitment will all play important roles in future ownership considerations.

⸻

6. What happens if BuyQK raises investment?

Investment enables BuyQK to grow, hire more talent, expand operations, and accelerate its mission.

Future ownership structures will continue to evolve responsibly while balancing the interests of founders, investors, employees, and the long-term success of the company.

⸻

7. What happens if BuyQK is acquired or merged?

Any future ownership program introduced by BuyQK will be subject to the legal agreements governing such programs and any applicable transaction terms.

Specific outcomes will depend upon the nature of the acquisition or merger and the governing legal documentation in effect at that time.

⸻

8. What happens if BuyQK never introduces an ESOP?

This document communicates BuyQK’s philosophy and future intent.

While BuyQK intends to explore formal ownership opportunities as it grows, no guarantee is made that an ESOP or any other ownership program will be introduced.

Business conditions, legal considerations, and strategic priorities may influence future decisions.

⸻

9. Can ownership be based only on designation?

No.

Leadership titles, designations, or seniority alone do not determine future ownership opportunities.

BuyQK believes that ownership should reflect sustained value creation, not hierarchy.

⸻

10. Why does BuyQK emphasize long-term commitment?

Building a meaningful company requires patience, resilience, and consistent effort.

Our ownership philosophy is designed to recognize individuals who continue contributing through different stages of the company’s journey rather than rewarding only initial participation.
